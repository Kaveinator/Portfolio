Details

Since the closure of Tanki X by AlternativaGames in early 2020, I have been actively involved in the Revive Tanki X community as a developer. Collaborating with other developers via GitHub, my responsibilities included various aspects of client development, including reverse engineering, patching, and maintenance of the game.

My expertise extended to frontend development, where I focused on reverse engineering the game client to implement fixes and introduce new content. This involved meticulous attention to detail and a deep understanding of game architecture.

During the initial stages of server development, I was tasked with creating a user database, user statistics, and logs. Although I utilized SQLite with ADO.NET for data storage, this implementation remained unfinished and was not deployed beyond the development stage due to time constraints.

Among my early contributions were the implementation of two new paint options, "Ocean Canyon" and "Cotton Candy," which expanded the game's customization features.

A significant milestone in my contributions was the creation of the Madness map, which introduced a new arena and enhanced gameplay dynamics. My experience as a community developer in Tanki X underscores my proficiency in Unity development, reverse engineering, and collaborative problem-solving.

As a disclaimer, I am not affiliated with AlternativaGames, the original developers of Tanki X, and I maintain no association with them at the time of writing.
